PChat is an iOS app that is sort of a blend of Yik-Yak and Tinychat. You can create chatrooms that are only accessable by people in a certain radius. Use it to talk to people in class during a boring lecture, see who's in the Library, create chatgroups for your club without getting everyones number, etc. The project was made in Xcode, and Parse was used for the back end.




Create Accounts

You can create accounts in the standard way.

Create Chatrooms

Hit the plus sign to create a chatroom. It will be centered at your current location.

See Available Chatrooms

All chatrooms in your vicinity will be displayed.

Chat With Strangers in Your Area

You can upload photos as well.

See Location

You can display the location of the chatroom as well.
